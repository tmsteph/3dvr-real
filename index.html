The Vision

We believe technology should help people become:

  • More independent
  • More creative
  • More connected
  • More organized
  • More empowered
  • More human

We are building an ecosystem of open-source tools, AI systems, educational resources, portable computing, and digital infrastructure designed for real people.

Not corporations. Not surveillance. Not endless subscriptions.

But a healthier relationship between humans and technology.
